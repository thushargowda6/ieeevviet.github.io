The IEEE student branch at VVIET, under IEEE Bangalore section is dedicated to serving the purpose of helping its members to enrich their technical knowledge and expertise.

The main focus of this association is to conduct social and technical activities for students and create awareness and encourage them to utilize the benefits of IEEE membership, including scholarships, competitions, and conference grants.

Having been active for over a decade, we have been honoured and humbled with numerous awards and accolades over time, including "The Best Small Student Chapter" of Region 10Â­ Bangalore. We organize and host a wide range of technical activities. We have our even sem fest fIEEEsta coming soon.

VVIET-IEEE conducts various kinds of workshops, seminars, webinars, technical talks, competitions, hackathons, and events througout the semester to help its members for the all-round development with the advancing technology.

The IEEE VVIET Student Branch connects engineers from various departments of the institute to come together, identify problem statements and act accordingly to improvise and promote Technical Activities which can in turn be forted for "Advancing Technology for Humanity".

Institute for Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an entity aimed at inculcating the sprirt of wisdom and knowledge. VVIET IEEE Student Branch was founded in 2009 under the guidance of our branch counselor, Dr. Bindu A Thomas. With events and workshops being conducted throughout the year, we make sure our members are kept up to date with the evolving needs of our technological world.

During the initial years, the student branch mainly focused on workshops and technical talks. Over the course of 10 years the student branch has grown immensely to what it is today. Our current chair is Mr.Thushar D .

Many students are actively contributing and involving themselves in various branch activities.
